Getting Started with Activity Feed

Share your experience by using the Activity Feed. Tap the Pencil icon to create a new post. By default, all posts will but if you re following other attendees, you can tap "Following" to only see posts from other attendees you are following.

No Posts Error

If events fail to load, first try swiping down to refresh the page. If you see "No More Posts..." you have seen all available posts, or need to tap "Everyone" to show posts from all attendees.
